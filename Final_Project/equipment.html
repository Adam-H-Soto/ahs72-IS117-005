Important Note:

As with any style of cooking, there are certain pieces of equipment that are needed to complete each recipe. Polish cooking, although mostly made by hand, also requires a few supplies atypical to other cuisines. The list to the right describes what supplies one may need before attempting certain recipes.

  • Polish Egg Beater / Whisk
  • Enamelware
  • Ricer
  • Handheld Flour Sifter
  • Stolnica (Cutting Board in shape of Trapezoid)
  • Kula do Ucierania (Stick with Ball on the end used for mixing) + Makutra Miska (Deep Stone Bowl with Ridges used for mixing)
